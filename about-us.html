who we are

Background

Jamiyah Global (Canada) is a Canadian-registered non-profit social service organisation dedicated to strengthening social, educational, spiritual, and humanitarian support for Muslim communities across North America, with a primary focus on Canada. Formerly known as Dunia Melayu Canada Communities (DMCC), the organisation is currently in the process of obtaining charitable status under the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

Jamiyah Global (Canada) aims to provide support to the wider Muslim community in North America, particularly Canada, through programmes in religious and secular education support, family development, mental health, youth empowerment, and social upliftment. A portion of funds raised in Canada will also support international humanitarian relief and development initiatives, such as disaster response and community resilience projects, implemented through compliant partnerships and global networks.

Its facility, the Jamiyah Global (Canada) Islamic Centre will serve as an inclusive and impactful hub for the broader Muslim community while also addressing specific needs of underserved groups, including Southeast Asian Muslims in Canada. Through strong governance, sustainable financial planning, and a commitment to service, Jamiyah Global (Canada) is positioned to become a transformative, multi-generational institution both locally and globally.

Vision

To uplift the Muslim communities, especially those underprivileged, in Canada and North America while contributing to global humanitarian and development needs.

Mission

To provide accessible social, educational, and spiritual support services that strengthen families, improve mental well-being, empower vulnerable Muslim communities, and advance humanitarian relief and long-term development efforts globally.

Core Values

Jamiyah Global (Canada) works to provide support and assistance to those in need, fostering a sense of community and promoting well-being. The organisation is committed to upholding values of compassion, generosity, and service to contribute positively to society.

Global Network

Since April 2016, Jamiyah Global (Canada) has served as the Canada Secretariat of Dunia Melayu Dunia Islam (DMDI), an organisation established in 2000 following the resolution of the 1st DMDI Convention chaired by TYT Tun Seri Setia (Dr.) Hj. Mohd Ali Mohd Rustam, President of DMDI. Founded under the auspices of the Melaka State Government, DMDI aims to strengthen unity and solidarity among Malay-Muslims while promoting cooperation in all areas of development between Malay-Muslim communities and nations around the world.

DMDI is the brainchild of TYT Tun Seri Setia (Dr.) Hj. Mohd Ali Mohd Rustam, who recognised the need for an effective platform to inspire Malay-Muslims globally to excel in key development sectors, such as economics, information technology, and education, thereby enhancing their well-being and helping uplift communities from poverty.

Dunia Melayu Canada Communities (DMCC), now known as Jamiyah Global (Canada), was launched by TYT Tun Seri Setia (Dr.) Hj. Mohd Ali Mohd Rustam on April 9th, 2016, in Mississauga, ON, Canada.

Legal & Governance Structure

Renaming of Dunia Melayu Canada Communities (DMCC) to Jamiyah Global (Canada) and application for Charitable Status under the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) for tax-deductible donations.
